GeoPol_News Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 Nov 2024

GeoPol_News ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy while you use our Telegram bot. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information when you use GeoPol_News:

  • Telegram Username: We collect your Telegram username to identify you and send updates.
  • Chat ID: We use your chat ID to send personalized news updates.
  • Feedback:Any feedback or suggestions you provide are collected to improve the bot's performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected from you for the following purposes:

  • To deliver global geopolitical news updates.
  • To customize the news delivery based on your preferences.
  • To improve the user experience by analyzing bot interactions and feedback.

3. Data Retention

We store your Telegram username and chat ID for as long as you subscribe to the bot’s updates. If you choose and send "\unsubscribe" command, we will delete your information.

4. Data Security

We use appropriate technical measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

5. Third-Party Services

GeoPol_News does not share your personal information with third parties. We may, however, use third-party services like Telegram’s servers to send you updates, but they will not use your information for their purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop receiving updates at any time by using the "\stop" command in the bot.
  • Request to delete your information from our records by using "\unsubscribe" command which you'll have to type manually in the bot.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the date at the top of the policy. By continuing to use the bot after these changes, you consent to the updated Privacy Policy.
